About Alexia

Hello, I'm Alexia.


I'm a Frontend Web Developer currently located in Vevey, Switzerland.

I had a career in the beauty industry as a hair stylist previously. I worked in Miami, Los Angeles, and Lausanne. When living in Los Angeles, I spent some time as a colorist and doing customer care at the hair color startup eSalon. It was during this time that my interest began to spark for web development.

I finished Le Wagon's 9-week Full Stack boot camp in April 2021. During my time there I totally became passionate about Frontend web development. I love the thrill of constructing a great looking site! Since completing the boot camp, I have continued to work on improving my skills. I am always looking for any opportunity to get experience to grow in my capabilities.

When I am not furthering my knowledge in code, you'll find me outside getting myself onto the mountains by hike or climb! I bring my pup Ruthie along as much as possible on my little adventures outdoors.
